Damage formula:

$$ \large \begin{align*} \mathrm{DMG_{out}} = \left[ \mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) + \mathrm{ATK_{flat}} \right] \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \times \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \right) \\ \end{align*} $$
  • A main stat (or 10 sub-stats) is worth either:
    • 43.20% of $\mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}$
    • 38.88% of $\mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}$
    • 32.40% of $\mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}$
    • 64.80% of $\mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}}$
$$ \large \begin{align*} \mathrm{Cost} = \frac{\mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}}{43.20\%} + \frac{\mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}}{38.88\%} + \frac{\mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}}{32.40\%} + \frac{\mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}}}{64.80\%} \end{align*}$$

Lagrange multiplier:

$$ \Large \begin{cases} \frac{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{out}}}{\partial \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}} = \lambda \frac{\partial \mathrm{Cost}}{\partial \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}} \\ \\ \frac{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{out}}}{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}} = \lambda \frac{\partial \mathrm{Cost}}{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}}} \\ \\ \frac{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{out}}}{\partial \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} = \lambda \frac{\partial \mathrm{Cost}}{\partial \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} \\ \\ \frac{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{out}}}{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} = \lambda \frac{\partial \mathrm{Cost}}{\partial \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} \end{cases} \implies \begin{cases} \mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \times \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \right) = \frac{\lambda}{43.20\%} && (1) \\ \\ \left[ \mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) + \mathrm{ATK_{flat}} \right] \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \times \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \right) = \frac{\lambda}{38.88\%} && (2) \\ \\ \left[ \mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) + \mathrm{ATK_{flat}} \right] \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) \times \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} = \frac{\lambda}{32.40\%} && (3) \\ \\ \left[ \mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) + \mathrm{ATK_{flat}} \right] \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) \times \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} = \frac{\lambda}{64.80\%} && (4) \end{cases} $$

(3) divided by (4):

$$ \large \begin{align*} \frac{\mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}}}{\mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} = \frac{64.80\%}{32.40\%} = 2 \quad\implies\quad \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} = 2 \times \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \end{align*}$$$$ \large \begin{align*} \text{Isolating } \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \text{ we have:} \qquad \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} &= \frac{1}{2} \times \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \end{align*}$$

(4) divided by (2):

$$ \large \begin{align*} \frac{\left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) \times \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}}{1 + \mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \times \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} = \frac{38.88\%}{64.80\%} = \frac{3}{5} \quad\implies\quad 5 \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) = 3 \times \left( \frac{1}{\mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} + \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \right) \end{align*}$$$$ \large \begin{align*} \text{Isolating } \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \text{ we have:} \qquad \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} &= \frac{3}{5} \times \left( \frac{1}{\mathrm{Rate_{crit}^{_{\%}}}} + \mathrm{DMG_{crit}^{_{\%}}} \right) - 1 \end{align*}$$

(2) divided by (1):

$$ \large \begin{align*} \frac{\mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) + \mathrm{ATK_{flat}}}{\mathrm{ATK_{base}} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right)} = \frac{43.20\%}{38.88\%} = \frac{10}{9} \quad\implies\quad 9 \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} + \frac{\mathrm{ATK_{flat}}}{\mathrm{ATK_{base}}} \right) = 10 \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) \end{align*}$$$$ \large \begin{align*} \text{Isolating } \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \text{ we have:} \qquad \mathrm{ATK_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} &= \frac{10}{9} \times \left( 1 + \mathrm{DMG_{bonus}^{_{\%}}} \right) - 1 - \frac{\mathrm{ATK_{flat}}}{\mathrm{ATK_{base}}} \end{align*}$$

import numpy as np
from matplotlib import pyplot

flat_ATK = 352.8
base_ATK = 1200

crit_DMG = np.linspace(0.5, 3.0, 1000)                     # Consider values of CRIT DAMAGE from 50% to 300%.
crit_Rate = np.minimum(1.0, crit_DMG/2)                    # From CRIT DAMAGE, compute optimal CRIT RATE (constraining it to 100%).
bonus_DMG = 3/5 * (crit_DMG + 1/crit_Rate) - 1             # From CRIT DAMAGE and CRIT RATE, compute optimal BONUS DAMAGE.
bonus_ATK = 10/9 * (1 + bonus_DMG) - 1 - flat_ATK/base_ATK # From BONUS DAMAGE, compute optimal BONUS ATTACK.

out_DMG = (base_ATK * (1 + bonus_ATK) + flat_ATK) * (1 + bonus_DMG) * (1 + crit_Rate * crit_DMG)
cost = bonus_ATK/0.4320 + bonus_DMG/0.3888 + crit_Rate/0.3240 + crit_DMG/0.6480

def plot(ax, x, y, xlabel, ylabel):
    ax.set_xlabel(xlabel, fontsize=14)
    ax.set_ylabel(ylabel, fontsize=14)
    ax.plot(x, y)
    ax.annotate('Low CRIT', (x[0], y[0]), xytext=(-12.0, 6.0), textcoords='offset pixels')
    ax.annotate('High CRIT', (x[-1], y[-1]), xytext=(-12.0, 6.0), textcoords='offset pixels')
    ax.grid()

fig, ax = pyplot.subplots(constrained_layout=True, figsize=(12, 12))
plot(ax, cost, out_DMG, r'$\mathrm{Cost}$', r'$\mathrm{DMG_{out}}$')
